森爸, 台灣街頭攝影師

Ethan Chiang，森爸，台灣街頭攝影師。

我是在韓國出生的華僑，曾居住於首爾，舊金山，目前定居於台灣，臺北。
我的長期目標是希望能能讓更多人認識街頭攝影，以及分享街頭攝影的資訊及技巧。
任何專案合作，攝影專案，訪談或問題，請隨時與我聯絡。

聯絡森爸

 

Hello world. My name is Ethan Chiang. A Taiwanese Street Photographer.

I am an overseas Taiwanese born and raised in Seoul, Korea. I have lived in Seoul, San Francisco and now based in Taipei, the capital of Taiwan. My goal is to spread the love and the knowledge of street photography to everyone.

For questions, commercial projects, assignment or workshops, please contact me anytime.
